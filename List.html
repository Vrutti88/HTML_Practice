B.Tech 1st Year Subject-wise Book List

Subject Overview

Mathematics
Involves topics like calculus, algebra, and differential equations.
Physics
Covers mechanics, optics, and electromagnetism.
Chemistry
Includes organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry concepts.
Programming
Focuses on learning programming languages like C or Python.
Engineering Drawing
Teaches basics of technical drawing and design.

Subjects and Recommended Books

  1. Mathematics
    • Advanced Engineering Mathematics – Erwin Kreyszig
    • Engineering Mathematics – B.S. Grewal
    • Calculus and Analytical Geometry – Thomas & Finney
  2. Physics
    • Engineering Physics – H.K. Malik & A.K. Singh
    • Concepts of Physics – H.C. Verma
    • Fundamentals of Physics – Halliday, Resnick, and Walker
  3. Chemistry
    • Engineering Chemistry – Jain & Jain
    • Physical Chemistry – P.W. Atkins
    • Organic Chemistry – Morrison & Boyd
  4. Programming
    • Let Us C – Yashavant Kanetkar
    • Programming in C – E. Balagurusamy
    • Python Crash Course – Eric Matthes
  5. Engineering Drawing
    • Engineering Drawing – N.D. Bhatt
    • Engineering Graphics – P.S. Gill
